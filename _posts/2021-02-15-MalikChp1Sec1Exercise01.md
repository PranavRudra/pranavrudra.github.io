## Solution:

$$
\begin{align*}
    A \cup B &= \{x, y, z, w\} \\
    A \cap B &= \{y \} \\
    A \setminus B &= \{x, z\} \\
    B \setminus A &= \{w\} \\
    A \times B &= \{(x, y), (x, w), (y, y), (y, w), (z, y), (z, w) \} \\
    \mathcal{P}(A) &= \{\emptyset, \{x\}, \{y\}, \{z\}, \{x, y\}, \{x, z\}, \{y, z\}, \{x, y, z\} \}
\end{align*}
$$

$$\qed$$
